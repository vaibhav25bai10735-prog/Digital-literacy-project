# Stay Safe Online: Checklist for College Students in India

## ✅ Safety Tips

-   Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor
    authentication (2FA).
-   Do not share OTPs (One-Time Passwords) or PINs with anyone, even if
    they claim to be from a bank.
-   Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them; scammers
    may send "collect requests" to trick you.
-   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions like online
    banking or UPI payments.
-   Check website URLs carefully before entering personal or financial
    information.
-   Do not click on suspicious links or download attachments from
    unknown emails or messages.
-   Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and
    antivirus software.
-   Log out from shared or public devices after use.

## 💰 Financial/UPI Safety Tips

-   Double-check the receiver's UPI ID before sending money.
-   Never scan unknown QR codes, especially those promising rewards or
    cashback.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

-   National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
-   Helpline: 1930
-   Report incidents immediately to prevent further damage.

## 📌 Reminder

Staying alert and cautious online can protect you from most cyber
threats.
