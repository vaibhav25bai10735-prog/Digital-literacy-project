# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## ✅ Do's

-   Think before you post.
-   Protect your personal information.
-   Use privacy settings wisely.
-   Be respectful and kind to others.
-   Verify information before sharing.

## ❌ Don'ts

-   Don't share sensitive personal details.
-   Don't engage in online arguments or harassment.
-   Don't post inappropriate or offensive content.
-   Don't overshare about your location or daily routine.
-   Don't believe or spread misinformation.
